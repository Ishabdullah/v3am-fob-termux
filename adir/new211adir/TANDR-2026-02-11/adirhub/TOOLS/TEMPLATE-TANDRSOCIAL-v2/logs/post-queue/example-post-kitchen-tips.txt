🔨 Kitchen Remodel Tips from the Pros 🔨

Thinking about updating your kitchen? Here are 3 things to consider BEFORE you start:

1️⃣ Layout First, Finishes Later
Your workflow matters more than granite colors. Think about how you move through your space - sink, stove, fridge (the work triangle). Get this right and your kitchen will be a joy to use for years.

2️⃣ Budget for Surprises
Set aside 15-20% for unexpected issues. Old homes especially can have surprises hiding behind those walls. Better to plan for it now than scramble later.

3️⃣ Think Long-Term
Trends come and go, but classic designs last. Choose finishes you'll love in 10 years, not just today. Your future self will thank you!
