CONTRACTOR DRAW SET UP/ FAQ 
DRAW SET UP: 
Within 72 hours of funding, the loan will be uploaded to our draw management system, Land Gorilla. The construction department at Churchill Mortgage will alert you when the file has been activated. A designated draw administrator will reach out to assist in setting up your login, covering draw request steps, and pointing out useful features of the site. The draw administrator will be your point of contact for draw requests, inspections, and general project tasks. For items outside of the above or for any other concerns please reach out to:  construction@churchillmortgage.com for immediate assistance. 
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS: 
What is Land Gorilla? 
Land Gorilla is the online draw management site where you can submit draw requests, review inspections/budgets, and upload draw documentation. Both the builder and borrower will be given access to the project. 
How long will it take to receive draw funds? 
Draws that require an inspection take approximately 5 business days to process the draw request and send funds. If no inspection is required, draw processing time is significantly shorter. 
How many draws can I take per month? 
By default, files are set up with a one per month draw frequency or to match stage draw count if noted in contract. Please alert Churchill construction team if a higher draw frequency is needed prior to close. 
Can I take a draw at close? 
Churchill Mortgage allows for builder/contractor to take a 10% draw at close to assist in placing materials deposits. If additional materials deposits are needed throughout the build, please note in draw request that no inspection is needed and upload corresponding invoice for review. For manufactured or kit homes please reach out to construction department for updated draw schedule.  
What lien waivers are needed? 
Builder/Contractor will receive a lien waiver, via Land Gorilla, to e-sign with each draw request.
