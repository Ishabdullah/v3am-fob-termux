🔗 Get Token from Graph Explorer

  1. Open Facebook Graph Explorer
  2. Select your Facebook Page (T & R Builder LLC)
  3. Click "Generate Access Token"
  4. Add permissions:
    • pages_read_engagement
    • pages_manage_posts
    • read_insights
  5. Copy the token and paste above

⏰ Token expires in ~1-2 hours. Get a new one when needed!
